Chandan Misra is an Assistant Professor II in the School of Computer Science & Engineering, XIM University. His research interests are in Parallel and Distributed Matrix Algebra, Sound and Music Computing, Computational Musicology, and Music Information Retrieval.

Prior to working as an Assistant Professor, he has worked as a Lecturer in the same university from June 2018 to November 2021. Before that, he obtained his Ph.D. and Master degree from IIT Kharagpur and Bachelor degree from Kalyani Govt. Engineering College affiliated by West Bengal University of Technology. He has two years industry experience at Wipro Technologies during 2007 and 2009 and has worked as a research intern at TCS Innovation Lab in 2016.
